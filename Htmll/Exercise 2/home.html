Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology ( An Autonomous Institution) is one of the best engineering colleges in Chennai and is part of Rajalakshmi Institutions, which has been synonymous with providing excellence in higher education to students for many years. Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology was established in 2008. RIT is accredited with highest grade of A++ by NAAC. RIT is affiliated with Anna University Chennai. It is one of the AICTE-approved colleges in Chennai New Delhi, and also offers NBA-approved courses.

To set a benchmark in the field of engineering education by providing quality technical education that fosters the spirit of learning, research and globally competent professionalism.
